About Me

I am currently transitioning from a stay at home father back into the IT. I took a few years off to help my son after he was diagnosed with ASD. Prior to that I spent 22 years in a helpdesk server support role. He has continued to grow and it is time to return back to a career I enjoy.

In my 22 years working in IT, I have been mostly self taught. I earned my A+ while attending Salt Lake Community College and began to work in desktop support. After a few years I moved up into different roles at the companies I worked for. Supporting different systems for Windows/Linux servers, warehouse automation, telecom systems, and personnel management.

I have held many positions and picked up a lot of skills along the way from basic PC and Server repair to mass machine deployment and management. My favorite was scripting. I was in charge of scripting for image, security, policy templating, installs,and uninstalls for hundreds of PC's and laptops for my company.
